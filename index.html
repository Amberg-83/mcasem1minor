About This Project

Nuclear disasters are catastrophic events that release harmful levels of radioactive material into the environment. Such incidents not only cause immediate destruction but also leave long-term environmental, social, and health impacts on future generations.

This project focuses on two major nuclear disasters: **Chernobyl** in Ukraine and **Fukushima** in Japan. Both incidents reshaped the global understanding of nuclear energy risks and pushed the world toward stronger safety standards. This website presents detailed information, simulation-based heatmaps, and image galleries to visualize the extent and impact of these events.
