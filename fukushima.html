Fukushima Daiichi Nuclear Accident (2011)

Cause of Disaster

A 9.0 magnitude earthquake followed by a massive tsunami caused a total power failure, leading to reactor meltdowns at the Fukushima Daiichi nuclear plant.

Deaths

Immediate radiation deaths: 0
Indirect disaster-related deaths: 1,000+ (evacuation stress, medical issues)

Major Pollutants Released

  • Cesium-134
  • Cesium-137
  • Iodine-131
