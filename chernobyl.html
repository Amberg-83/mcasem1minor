CHERNOBYL — 1986

Overview & Context

On 26 April 1986 Reactor 4 at the Chernobyl Nuclear Power Plant exploded during a late-night safety test...

The accident combined reactor design flaws and operator errors. The explosion released massive radioactive material...

Long-term consequences include an exclusion zone, increased cancer cases, and worldwide nuclear policy changes.
